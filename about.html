It's really just about this one guy

滔, pronounced Tāo, means a massive wave of water. 饕, also pronounced Tāo, means a glutton. Together:

滔饕
A big ass glutton, yes

TaoTao aims to bring Chinese food culture, knowledge, and history to the English speaking audience. TaoTao is like your storytelling grandmother, a teacher passionate about passing down knowledge to his or her chef pupils, or simply your food-obsessed friend taking you out to his favourate hole-in-the-wall dumpling house.
